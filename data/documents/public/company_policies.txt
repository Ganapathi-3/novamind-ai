TECHNOVA INC. — COMPANY POLICIES & GUIDELINES
==============================================
Department: Public
Access: All Staff

WORK FROM HOME POLICY
---------------------
TechNova supports flexible remote work for all employees.

Core hours: All employees must be available online between 10 AM and 4 PM in their local timezone.
Full remote: Employees may work fully remote with manager approval.
Hybrid option: Office attendance is encouraged on Tuesdays and Thursdays.
Equipment: The company provides a laptop, monitor, and headset for all remote employees.
Internet allowance: Remote employees receive 1000 rupees per month as internet reimbursement.

LEAVE POLICY
------------
Casual Leave: 12 days per year (cannot be carried forward)
Sick Leave: 10 days per year (requires medical certificate for more than 2 consecutive days)
Earned Leave: 15 days per year (can be carried forward up to 30 days)
Maternity Leave: 26 weeks paid leave as per government regulations
Paternity Leave: 10 days paid leave
Bereavement Leave: 5 days for immediate family members
National Holidays: All employees get 10 national and festival holidays per year

To apply for leave, submit a request through the HR portal at least 2 days in advance.
For emergency leaves, inform your manager directly via phone or WhatsApp.

CODE OF CONDUCT
---------------
TechNova expects all employees to maintain the highest standards of professional behavior.

Respect: Treat all colleagues, clients, and vendors with respect regardless of their position.
Punctuality: Attend all scheduled meetings on time. Notify in advance if you cannot attend.
Confidentiality: Never share company data, client information, or internal documents outside the organization.
Social media: Do not post about clients, internal projects, or company financials on social media.
Conflict of interest: Disclose any potential conflicts of interest to your manager immediately.
Harassment: Zero tolerance for any form of workplace harassment. Report to HR immediately.

COMMUNICATION TOOLS
-------------------
Slack: Primary messaging tool for all internal communication
Email: For formal communication and client correspondence
Zoom: For video meetings and team calls
Notion: For documentation and project tracking
Jira: For task and sprint management

OFFICE TIMINGS
--------------

Cafeteria: 8 AM to 8 PM on working days

TRAVEL POLICY
-------------
Domestic travel: Book at least 3 days in advance through the travel portal
International travel: Requires VP approval and must be booked 2 weeks in advance
Daily allowance (domestic): 1500 rupees per day
Daily allowance (international): 50 USD per day
All travel expenses must be submitted with receipts within 7 days of return.
