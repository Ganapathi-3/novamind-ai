TECHNOVA INC. — NEW EMPLOYEE ONBOARDING GUIDE
==============================================
Department: Public
Access: All Staff

WELCOME TO TECHNOVA
-------------------
We are thrilled to have you on board! This guide will help you get settled in your first 30 days.
Please read it carefully and reach out to your buddy or HR if you have any questions.

YOUR FIRST DAY CHECKLIST
-------------------------
1. Collect your laptop and access card from IT (Ground Floor, Room 101)
2. Set up your company email: firstname.lastname@technova.com
3. Join the company Slack workspace using your company email
4. Complete the mandatory security awareness training on the learning portal
5. Fill and submit your joining documents to HR (Room 203)
6. Meet your assigned buddy who will guide you through your first two weeks
7. Attend the welcome lunch with your team at 1 PM

FIRST WEEK ACTIVITIES
---------------------
Day 1: Company orientation with HR, IT setup, meet your manager
Day 2: Team introductions, tools setup (Slack, Notion, Jira, GitHub)
Day 3: Shadow your buddy, understand team workflows and ongoing projects
Day 4: First project briefing with your manager, set 30-day goals
Day 5: Weekly team standup, retrospective, and one-on-one with manager

30-60-90 DAY PLAN
-----------------
First 30 days:
  - Understand the product, team structure, and codebase
  - Complete all mandatory trainings
  - Deliver your first small feature or analysis
  - Build relationships with immediate team members

First 60 days:
  - Own a module or deliverable independently
  - Participate actively in sprint planning and retrospectives
  - Contribute to documentation and knowledge base

First 90 days:
  - Deliver a meaningful project milestone
  - Share learnings with the team
  - Set 6-month goals with your manager

LEARNING AND DEVELOPMENT
------------------------
Each employee gets 2000 rupees per year for courses, books, and certifications.
Approved platforms: Coursera, Udemy, Pluralsight, O'Reilly, LinkedIn Learning
To claim: Submit the invoice and course completion certificate to hr@technova.com
Internal workshops: Held every 2nd Friday of the month — check Slack for schedule

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
---------------------------
Q: When will I receive my ID card?
A: ID cards are issued within 3 working days of joining.

Q: How do I apply for leave in my first month?
A: Use the HR portal at hr.technova.com. Leave accrues from day one.

Q: How do I raise a purchase request?
A: Fill the purchase request form on Notion and get your manager's approval.

Q: Can I work from home in my first month?
A: First 2 weeks require in-office presence. After that, hybrid is available.

Q: Who do I contact for laptop issues?
A: Email it@technova.com or walk in to Room 101 on the Ground Floor.
