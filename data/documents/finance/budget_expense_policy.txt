EXPENSE APPROVAL LIMITS
------------------------
Below 5000 rupees: No approval required, submit receipt within 7 days
5000 to 25000 rupees: Manager approval required before spending
25000 to 1 Lakh rupees: VP approval required before spending
1 Lakh to 5 Lakhs rupees: CEO or CFO approval required
Above 5 Lakhs rupees: Board approval required

All approved expenses must be submitted in the expense management system (Zoho Expense).
Receipts are mandatory for all expenses above 500 rupees.
Cash expenses above 2000 rupees are not reimbursed — use company card.

VENDOR PAYMENTS
---------------
Standard payment terms: Net 30 days from invoice date
Early payment discount: Negotiate 2 percent discount for Net 7 payments
Minimum 3 vendor quotes required for any purchase above 1 Lakh rupees
All vendor contracts above 5 Lakhs must be reviewed by the legal team
Preferred payment method: NEFT or RTGS (no cash payments to vendors)
