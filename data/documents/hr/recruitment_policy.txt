TECHNOVA INC. — RECRUITMENT AND HIRING POLICY
=============================================
Department: HR
Access: HR Staff Only
Confidential Document

HIRING PROCESS OVERVIEW
------------------------
All hiring at TechNova follows a standardized process to ensure fair, unbiased,
and efficient recruitment across all departments.

JOB REQUISITION
---------------
Step 1: Hiring manager submits a Job Requisition Form (JRF) to HR via the HR portal
Step 2: HR verifies headcount budget with Finance department
Step 3: VP of the department approves the requisition
Step 4: HR creates the job description using approved templates
Step 5: Position is posted internally for 5 business days first (internal mobility preference)
Step 6: If no internal candidate, position is posted externally

Approved external job portals: LinkedIn, Naukri, Indeed, AngelList, Instahyre

INTERVIEW PROCESS
-----------------
For Engineering and Data Science roles (5 rounds):
  Round 1: HR screening call (30 minutes) — culture fit, motivation, communication
  Round 2: Technical screening (60 minutes) — core skills assessment
  Round 3: Take-home assignment (48 hours to complete)
  Round 4: Technical deep dive with team (90 minutes)
  Round 5: Leadership and culture interview with manager and VP (45 minutes)

For Non-Technical roles (3 rounds):
  Round 1: HR screening call (30 minutes)
  Round 2: Skills and experience interview with hiring manager (60 minutes)
  Round 3: Case study and culture fit with team lead (45 minutes)

Interview feedback must be submitted on the HR portal within 24 hours of each round.

OFFER AND NEGOTIATION
---------------------
Offers are made only after unanimous hiring committee approval.
HR makes the verbal offer first before issuing a written offer letter.
Offer letter is sent within 2 business days of verbal acceptance.
Standard notice period for new hires: 2 months from joining date.
Salary negotiation is permissible within the approved band for that level.
Maximum salary offered to freshers: Mid-level band lower limit.

BACKGROUND VERIFICATION
-----------------------
All offers are conditional on background verification (BGV):
  - Identity verification (Aadhaar or Passport)
  - Education certificate verification
  - Previous employment verification (last 5 years)
  - Criminal record check

BGV is conducted by a third-party agency. Results expected within 10 business days.
If BGV fails, the offer may be withdrawn with proper notice.

REFERRAL PROGRAM
----------------
Employees can refer candidates through the HR portal.
Referral bonus paid after the referred candidate completes 90 days:
  Junior level hire: 10000 rupees
  Mid-level hire: 20000 rupees
  Senior level hire: 35000 rupees
  Lead or above: 50000 rupees

Only one referral bonus per hire. Self-referrals are not eligible.

DIVERSITY AND INCLUSION
-----------------------
TechNova is committed to building a diverse workforce.
All job descriptions must use gender-neutral language.
At least one diverse candidate must be shortlisted for every position.
Hiring panels must include at least one woman or underrepresented group member.
Blind resume screening is used for engineering roles to reduce bias.

INTERNSHIP HIRING
-----------------
Internships are 2 to 6 months in duration.
Interns are paid a stipend of 15000 to 25000 rupees per month depending on role.
Pre-placement offers (PPO) are extended to top-performing interns.
PPO conversion rate target: 30 percent of interns per batch.

Contact: talent@technova.com for recruitment related queries.
