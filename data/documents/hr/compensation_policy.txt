HR CONFIDENTIAL — Compensation Policy

Salary Bands (Annual USD):
  Intern:     20000 to 35000
  Junior:     65000 to 85000
  Mid-Level:  85000 to 120000
  Senior:     120000 to 160000
  Director:   220000 to 300000

Annual Performance Bonus:
  Meets expectations:   5 percent of base salary
  Exceeds expectations: 10 percent of base salary
  Outstanding:          Up to 20 percent of base salary

Benefits:
  - Medical, dental, vision 100 percent covered for employee
  - 401k with 4 percent company match
  - 2000 dollars per year learning and development budget
  - Unlimited PTO with 15 days minimum recommended
  - 12 weeks paid parental leave

Referral Bonus:
  Junior or Mid level hire: 2000 dollars
  Senior or Lead hire: 5000 dollars
  Director or above: 10000 dollars

Confidential — HR staff only. Do not share outside HR department.
