TECHNOVA INC. — PERFORMANCE REVIEW PROCESS
==========================================
Department: HR
Access: HR Staff Only
Confidential Document

OVERVIEW
--------
TechNova conducts structured performance reviews twice a year to ensure employees grow,
receive fair compensation, and align with company goals.

REVIEW CYCLE
------------
Mid-Year Review: July 1 to July 31 (covers January to June performance)
Annual Review: January 1 to January 31 (covers July to December performance)
Probation Review: At end of 3-month probation period (for new joiners only)

Performance ratings are awarded on a 5-point scale:
  5 - Exceptional: Far exceeds all expectations
  4 - Exceeds Expectations: Consistently surpasses goals
  3 - Meets Expectations: Achieves all set goals
  2 - Needs Improvement: Partially meets goals
  1 - Unsatisfactory: Does not meet expectations

COMPENSATION REVIEW
-------------------
Annual increment is linked directly to performance rating:

Rating 5 (Exceptional):        12 to 18 percent salary increment
Rating 4 (Exceeds):            8 to 12 percent salary increment
Rating 3 (Meets Expectations): 5 to 8 percent salary increment
Rating 2 (Needs Improvement):  0 to 3 percent salary increment
Rating 1 (Unsatisfactory):     No increment, performance improvement plan initiated

Increments are effective from April 1 each year.
Promotion decisions are made separately and may include additional salary revisions.

PROMOTION CRITERIA
------------------
To be considered for promotion an employee must:
  - Have completed minimum 18 months in the current role
  - Have a minimum rating of 4 in the most recent two reviews
  - Have demonstrated ownership of projects beyond their current scope
  - Have a recommendation from their direct manager
  - Pass a promotion panel interview with department head and HR

Promotion levels:
  Junior → Mid-Level: 18 months minimum
  Mid-Level → Senior: 24 months minimum
  Senior → Lead/Staff: 30 months minimum + technical presentation
  Lead → Manager: Separate management track assessment required

PERFORMANCE IMPROVEMENT PLAN (PIP)
-----------------------------------
A PIP is initiated for employees with rating 1 or two consecutive rating 2 reviews.

PIP duration: 60 to 90 days
PIP includes: Specific measurable goals, bi-weekly check-ins with manager and HR
Outcome options:
  - Successfully completes PIP: Continues employment, reviewed in 3 months
  - Does not complete PIP: Employment may be terminated with notice period

REVIEW PROCESS STEPS
---------------------
Step 1: Employee completes self-assessment on HR portal (first 7 days of review month)
Step 2: Manager reviews self-assessment and adds manager comments (days 8 to 14)
Step 3: Skip-level manager reviews and approves (days 15 to 18)
Step 4: HR calibration session across all departments (days 19 to 21)
Step 5: Manager delivers feedback to employee in one-on-one meeting (days 22 to 28)
Step 6: Employee acknowledges review on HR portal (days 29 to 31)

GOAL SETTING
------------
Each employee must set 4 to 6 SMART goals at the start of each review period.
Goals must be agreed upon by both the employee and manager and logged in Notion.

Goal categories:
  - Business impact goals (50 percent weightage)
  - Technical or skill development goals (30 percent weightage)
  - Collaboration and culture goals (20 percent weightage)

CONFIDENTIALITY
---------------
All performance ratings, salary information, and PIP details are strictly confidential.
HR staff must not discuss individual ratings outside of formal review processes.
Breach of confidentiality is a serious misconduct and may result in disciplinary action.

Contact: hr-director@technova.com for any questions about the review process.
