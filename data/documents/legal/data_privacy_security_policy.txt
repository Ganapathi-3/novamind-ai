TECHNOVA INC. — DATA PRIVACY AND SECURITY POLICY
=================================================
Department: Legal
Access: Admin Only
Confidential Document

OVERVIEW
--------
TechNova is committed to protecting personal data in compliance with applicable data
protection laws including India's Digital Personal Data Protection Act 2023 (DPDPA),
GDPR (for EU customer data), and other regional regulations.

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, vendors, and partners who process
personal data on behalf of TechNova.

DATA CLASSIFICATION
-------------------
TechNova classifies data into four categories:

Level 1 — Public:
  Definition: Information intended for public consumption
  Examples: Marketing materials, public website content, press releases
  Handling: No restrictions on sharing

Level 2 — Internal:
  Definition: General business information not meant for external audiences
  Examples: Internal policies, meeting notes, employee directories
  Handling: Share only with employees, do not send to external parties

Level 3 — Confidential:
  Definition: Sensitive business information with restricted access
  Examples: Salary data, performance reviews, customer contracts, financial reports
  Handling: Share only with authorized personnel, encrypt when transmitting

Level 4 — Restricted:
  Definition: Highly sensitive data with strict access controls
  Examples: Customer PII, authentication credentials, source code, fundraising data
  Handling: Encrypt at rest and in transit, audit all access, need-to-know basis only

PERSONAL DATA HANDLING
-----------------------
Personal data includes: Name, email, phone number, address, financial details,
device identifiers, location data, biometric data, health information.

Employees must:
  - Collect only the minimum data necessary for the business purpose
  - Store personal data only in approved systems (not personal drives or email)
  - Never share personal data with unauthorized third parties
  - Report any suspected data breach to security@technova.com within 2 hours
  - Delete personal data when it is no longer needed for its purpose

Data retention periods:
  Customer data: 5 years after contract end
  Employee data: 7 years after employment ends
  Transaction data: 10 years (regulatory requirement)
  Marketing data: 2 years from last interaction
  Application logs: 90 days

SECURITY REQUIREMENTS
---------------------
Password policy:
  Minimum 12 characters, must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and symbols
  Password rotation every 90 days for admin accounts
  No password reuse for last 10 passwords
  Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is mandatory for all employees

Device security:
  Company laptops must have full-disk encryption enabled
  Screen lock activates after 5 minutes of inactivity
  No installation of unapproved software on company devices
  Lost or stolen devices must be reported to IT within 1 hour

Network security:
  Use company VPN when accessing internal systems remotely
  Do not connect to public WiFi without VPN
  Guest WiFi at office is for visitors only — employees must use corporate network
  Never connect personal devices to the corporate network

EMAIL AND COMMUNICATION:
  Do not send confidential data via personal email
  Encrypt email attachments containing Level 3 or Level 4 data
  Be alert for phishing emails — report suspicious emails to security@technova.com
  Never click links or download attachments from unknown senders

INCIDENT RESPONSE
-----------------
If you suspect a data breach or security incident:

Step 1: Do not try to investigate or fix it yourself
Step 2: Immediately report to security@technova.com and call +91 9876509999
Step 3: Preserve evidence — do not delete files or logs
Step 4: The Security team will assess severity and lead the response
Step 5: Regulatory notification within 72 hours if customer data is involved (GDPR)
Step 6: Post-incident report and remediation plan within 14 days

THIRD PARTY AND VENDOR SECURITY
---------------------------------
All vendors who process TechNova data must sign a Data Processing Agreement (DPA).
Vendor security assessments required annually for critical vendors.
Customer data must never be stored on vendor systems without explicit approval.
AI and LLM API usage: Customer PII must never be sent to external AI APIs.

EMPLOYEE OBLIGATIONS
--------------------
All employees must:
  - Complete annual data privacy and security training
  - Sign the Data Protection and Confidentiality Agreement on joining
  - Report policy violations without fear of retaliation
  - Understand that violations may result in disciplinary action including termination

RIGHTS OF DATA SUBJECTS
-----------------------
Individuals whose personal data we hold have the right to:
  - Access their personal data
  - Correct inaccurate data
  - Request deletion of their data
  - Object to processing
  - Data portability

All data subject requests must be forwarded to privacy@technova.com within 24 hours.
We must respond to data subject requests within 30 days.

Contact: legal@technova.com or privacy@technova.com for data privacy queries.
